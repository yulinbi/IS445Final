Now! Welcome our main character

In this visualization, I give people three choice to select and explore in each choice the rank of total salary in Illinois. When people select agency, they will find which agency pays employees more. If they choose some of the better majors summarized before, they can go to these agencies to apply for their corresponding positions; when people select position title, they will find which position gets more payment. And they can combine this information and previous information to help them choose major to learn. As for selecting year, people can explore the salary is increasing in these years and they should be optimistic when they find jobs in the future because the salary will also increase.
